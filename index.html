General Overview

Project Goal: Bathroom renovation. Please note all measurements below are in inches.

Shower Zone Layout

  • Width: ~35 inches.
  • Bench: No bench inside the shower.
  • Shower Half-Wall: Must include a hidden niche from the inside, shower controls, and an access panel (hatch) from the outside.

Vanity / Sink Layout

  • Width: 30 inch vanity.
  • Gap: Leave a small gap between the vanity and the shower glass/wall (do not install flush against the glass).

Toilet

  • Wall-hung Toto toilet.
