Bathroom Renovation Project

General Overview

NOTE: Electrical work will be fully prepped ahead of time. Please do not include electrical rough-in/prep in your quote.
Proper renderings and layout drawings are pending.

Project Goal: Bathroom renovation.

Overall Scope of Work Needed

  • Materials: Owner will provide all finish materials (tiles, vanity, toilet, fixtures, lighting, door).
  • Demolition and removal of existing fixtures as needed.
  • Plumbing rough-in and modifications.
  • Tiling (floor and walls).
  • Build a platform for the washer and dryer in the basement.
  • Relocate the washer and dryer to the basement.
  • Installation of new bathroom fixtures, shower hardware, vanity, and toilet.
  • Install new pre-hung door.
  • Install recessed medicine cabinet.
  • Drywall repair, finishing, and painting.

Shower Zone Layout

  • Width: ~35 inches.
  • Bench: No bench inside the shower.
  • Shower Half-Wall: Must include a hidden niche from the inside, and shower controls.
  • Fixtures: Rain showerhead plus a handheld wand on a slide bar.

Vanity / Sink Layout

  • Width: 30 inch vanity.
  • Faucet: Faucet mounted on the counter.

Toilet

  • Wall-hung Toto toilet. Include plumbing for bidet seat.
